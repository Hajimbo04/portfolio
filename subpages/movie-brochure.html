Super Nasi Odyssey

Project Manager Gameplay Programmer

Project Overview

Gasing Guardian is a 3D physics-based platformer created in Unity. The game blends traditional Malaysian culture with fast-paced, modern gameplay. Players control a hero armed with a "gasing" (a traditional top) which functions as a versatile grappling hook, combat weapon, and puzzle-solving tool.

My Contributions

As the Project Manager, I led a team of 5, organizing sprints, managing tasks via Trello, and ensuring we met all our milestones for this academic capstone project.

As the lead Gameplay Programmer, my primary responsibilities included:

  • Physics-Based Grappling Hook: Engineered the core gasing mechanic from scratch, allowing for swinging, pulling, and aiming with a predictable arc.
  • Player Combat System: Developed the state machine for player combat, including melee strikes with the gasing, a ranged throw-and-recall ability, and interaction with physics objects.
  • Enemy AI: Programmed the basic AI for ground-based enemies, including patrol states, player detection, and a "stunned" state when hit by the gasing.
